Our Story

About Miorah

Our Journey

Born from a Passion for Style

Miorah Boutique began with a simple belief — that every woman deserves to feel extraordinary in what she wears. Founded in the heart of Bengaluru, we set out to bridge the gap between high-end fashion and personal expression.

What started as a small studio with a handful of dedicated artisans has grown into a boutique known for its impeccable craftsmanship, attention to detail, and deeply personal approach to dressing women for every milestone in their lives.

From bridal lehengas to everyday kurtas, from boardroom-ready ensembles to celebration wear — Miorah crafts each piece with the same love and precision that defines our identity.
